Why I Got Into Coaching

It allows me to escape from my actual life. Throughout my Junior and Senior years of high school, I found myself feeling not motivated to do anything. I felt like there was so much pressure on making sure that I got recruited to a good school and play well in my sports. When I started coaching, I found peace and felt distracted from all of my worries. This escape is what has kept me coming back, along with the joy I feel from making connections with the kids. I have also realized that I am better in my sports because I am re-emphasizing the basic principles in my life as I teach the kids.
